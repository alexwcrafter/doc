Authorized substitutes
======================

Substitutes are user accounts that are authorized to act on behalf of an other user to validate or refuse a ticket or a change.

The user can choose one or several accounts to delegate the validation task. He may optionnaly limit the date range of the delegation by setting one or both date lmimits.

The user may be also a delegatee of an other user. In this case, he will be see in a table his delegators and the date range of each delegation.
